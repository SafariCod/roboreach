About Roboreach

Mission-driven robotics access for students in our community.

Roboreach Foundation is a Florida-based 501(c)(3) nonprofit serving Doral families through practical, mentorship-led robotics education with VEX IQ and VEX V5 for students who lack access due to cost or limited school support, and students seeking proven, competitive-level mentorship and structured technical growth.

Mission

Our mission is to serve students who lack access due to cost or limited school support, and students seeking proven, competitive-level mentorship and structured technical growth through hands-on robotics instruction and consistent training.

Program Model

The same office space in Doral hosts both our 4-week summer camp and year-round training hub, creating continuity from beginner fundamentals to competition readiness.
