Donate & Sponsor

Help students access STEM tools, mentorship, and robotics training.

Your support funds hands-on instruction, VEX IQ and VEX V5 equipment, and our Doral training space for students who lack access due to cost or limited school support, and students seeking proven, competitive-level mentorship and structured technical growth. Roboreach Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3), and donations are tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law.

Sponsor & Tax Information
