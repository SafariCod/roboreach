Practical robotics training for students who need greater STEM access.

Roboreach Foundation provides office-based robotics learning with VEX IQ and VEX V5 through a 4-week summer camp and a year-round training hub. We serve students who lack access due to cost or limited school support, and students seeking proven, competitive-level mentorship and structured technical growth.

What We Do

We support students who lack access due to cost or limited school support, and students seeking proven, competitive-level mentorship and structured technical growth.

4-Week Summer Camp

A structured office-based camp where students learn robot build fundamentals, programming, and team collaboration.

Year-Round Training Hub

The same Doral office space supports recurring practice, mentorship, and development beyond summer.

Mentorship & Readiness

Mentors with state-level VEX experience coach students in engineering habits, communication, and competitive preparation.

Program Pathway

Access to tools and mentorship, then skills development, then competition readiness.

1

Access

Students enter a consistent office-based learning environment with guided support and equipment.

2

Build Skills

Students train in robot design, coding, sensors, troubleshooting, and team responsibilities.

3

Compete

Students prepare for official competition participation as Roboreach builds teams through the camp-to-team pipeline.

Current Season Highlights

State-Level Mentor Experience Mentorship includes competitors with strong VEX experience, including state-level results.
VEX IQ + VEX V5 Pathway Training is designed to support progression from IQ fundamentals to V5 readiness.
Qualified to States Mentors include experienced VEX competitors from teams that have currently qualified to States.
