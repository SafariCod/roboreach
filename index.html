Building STEM access through robotics, mentorship, and competition.

Roboreach Foundation empowers students who lack access to STEM due to cost or limited school support, using VEX IQ and VEX V5 in a hands-on, office-based training environment.

What We Do

A focused robotics pathway that combines access, instruction, and competition experience.

4-Week Summer Camp

A structured camp at our office with hands-on build and programming instruction using VEX platforms.

Year-Round Competitive Teams

Students continue practicing and developing through recurring team sessions and competition preparation.

Mentorship & Skills

Mentors teach building, coding, iteration, teamwork, and strategy in a real competitive setting.

Program Pathway

Access → Build Skills → Compete

1

Access

Students gain access to an equipped office-based robotics environment and guided instruction.

2

Build Skills

They learn building fundamentals, programming, sensors, troubleshooting, and team communication.

3

Compete

Students apply their skills in VEX IQ and VEX V5 team practice and competition season events.
