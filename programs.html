Programs & Training Model

Office-based robotics training from first build to competitive team development.

Roboreach Foundation runs a 4-week summer camp hosted at our office, and the same office is used year-round for team practice and development.

4-Week Summer Camp

Hands-on build + programming instruction using VEX IQ and VEX V5 in a structured office-based setting.

Week 1: Intro, Safety, Building Basics

  • Program introduction and team expectations
  • Safety habits and workspace procedures
  • Building fundamentals and part familiarity

Week 2: Programming + Sensors

  • Programming fundamentals and robot control logic
  • Sensor integration and testing
  • Debugging basics and repeatable practice

Week 3: Strategy + Iteration + Skills Runs

  • Game strategy and task prioritization
  • Robot iteration based on performance feedback
  • Timed skills runs and improvement tracking

Week 4: Scrimmage + Team Formation

  • Scrimmage practice and role coordination
  • Competition-style routines and teamwork
  • Team formation for year-round development

Year-Round Teams

Students continue in competitive teams with recurring practices at our office, focused on robot reliability, programming progress, strategy, and match readiness throughout the season.

Roboreach Foundation is currently competing in both VEX IQ and VEX V5, giving students ongoing exposure to real competition workflows.

Practice Cadence & Roles

Practice sessions combine technical work, strategy discussion, and performance review as competitions approach.

Build Code Drive Strategy

Students can rotate and specialize to strengthen both team depth and individual growth.

Office as Training Center: The same Doral office space supports summer instruction and year-round team practice and development.
